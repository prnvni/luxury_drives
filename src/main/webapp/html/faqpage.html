General

What makes your luxury car rentals different?

We don't just rent cars — we deliver experiences. Every vehicle in our fleet is meticulously maintained, fully insured, and paired with a concierge-style service from the moment you book until the moment you return.

Where are you located?

We're based in Dubai, with delivery options to hotels, airports, and private residences.

Can I rent a car if I'm visiting from abroad?

Absolutely. Just bring a valid international driving license, passport, and credit card. Our team will handle the rest and help make the process seamless.

Booking and Payment

How do I book a car?

Choose your car, select your rental dates, and click Reserve Now. You'll receive instant confirmation and a personal concierge contact to assist with logistics.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards and international debit cards, Apple Pay, Google Pay, and wire transfers for long-term or corporate bookings.

Do I need to pay a deposit?

Yes — a refundable security deposit is required depending on the car category. The deposit is released after the vehicle is inspected following your rental.

Can I cancel or change my booking?

Free cancellations or modifications are allowed up to 24 hours before your booking starts. Changes inside that window may incur a fee depending on the vehicle and timing.

Cars & Policies

What cars do you offer?

Our fleet includes supercars (Lamborghini, McLaren, Ferrari), luxury sedans (Rolls-Royce, Bentley, Mercedes S-Class), convertibles, and premium SUVs. Each vehicle is curated for performance, comfort, and prestige.

Are there mileage limits?

All rentals include a generous daily mileage allowance. You can upgrade to an unlimited mileage option at checkout if you plan to drive further.

Can I choose the exact model and color?

Yes — you may select your preferred model and color from our live inventory. We confirm the exact car during booking; if an identical configuration is unavailable, our concierge will propose a comparable replacement.

What about fuel and insurance?

Vehicles are delivered fueled. You are charged only for fuel used. Comprehensive insurance is included; coverage details depend on the vehicle category and will be shown at booking.

Experience and Support

Do you deliver and pick up the cars?

Yes. We offer complimentary delivery within city limits and premium airport delivery for a seamless handover. Pickup and delivery times are coordinated by your personal concierge.

Can I rent with a driver or chauffeur?

Professional chauffeurs are available for VIP events, weddings, corporate needs, or any booking where you'd prefer to be driven. Chauffeur services are bookable at checkout.

What if I need help during my rental?

Our 24/7 support team is available by phone, WhatsApp, or live chat for assistance with route advice, roadside support, or any emergency needs.

Do you offer special experiences or packages?

Yes — from curated weekend getaways to private track days and photoshoot packages. Visit the Custom Packages page or contact concierge for tailored experiences.

Didn't find what you're looking for?

Our concierge team is ready to help you personally. Whether it's a special request, an event booking, or a bespoke package — reach out and we'll handle everything.
